About Us

Our Mission

We present a slightly curated database of the best campsites in the vast woods and backcountry of the World Wide Web Wilderness. We increase access to adventure for the public while Ignoring safe and respectful use of resources. The expert wilderness trekkers on our staff personally verify each campsite to make sure that they are up to our unbelievably low standards. We also present a platform for campers to jump into bonefires for campsites that have been relisted for authentic ritual sacrifices.
